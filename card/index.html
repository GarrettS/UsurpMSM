Infocards

Infocards are business cards that have concise information on an issue that is being covered up or misrepresented by mainstream media. Give infocards to friends, strangers, and anyone who isn't part of your internet circles who might not know about these important media coverups.

For example, approching a stranger:

You: "Hey did you hear about NDAA; just signed into law?"
Stranger: "No, what's that?"
You: "It's a bill just passed by Congress and the President that allows anyone to be arrested and detained, indefinitely,without charges our trial."
You: "You won't see it on any news. Please search for yourself."
You: [hands card]

Printing Infocards

  1. save the PDF files below to your computer.
  2. Contact a print service and ask if they print double-sided 2.5 x 3" business cards.
  3. They will ask you to send them the PDF files.
  4. Send them the files you saved to your computer.

How to order from MBMLLC.net

  1. Go to MBMLLC.net and create an account.
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Select business cards, select upload design
  4. select options in drop down boxes, size Us Standard, Full color both sides, Select 14 pt paper stock, Select quantity, Select other options, If you select instant online proof there will be a discount.
  5. enter shipping zip for estimated shipping
  6. Click agree to terms, initial in box and click continue
  7. On next screen select portrait
  8. upload files for front and back in appropriate browse boxes, click upload, upload may take a few seconds to initialize
    • Front: ndaa-front.pdf
    • Back: ndaa-back.pdf
  9. Once files are loaded the screen will change so you can review your files if you selected instant proof option. Initial approval of files and click add to cart.
  10. Screen will change to shopping cart. Fill out billing, shipping, and coupon code "SCGProject" for a 50% off. You should see the discount in red under the subtotal. If not, click APPLY again. If you enter the code and then make changes to the order, you will need to re-click "apply". This code is good only for StormCloudsGathering project and approved affiliates.
