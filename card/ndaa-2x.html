NDAA

S. 1867, National Defense Authorization Act

NDAA

Passed by Congress, then signed by President Obama on Dec 31, 2011.

S. 1021. Allows US Armed forces to arrest and detain anyone, including Americans, and hold them indefinitely, without charge or trial.

This violates habeas corpus, bill of rights, and posse comitatus.

Taxpayer Cost: $662 billion
